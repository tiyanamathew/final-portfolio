hi there!

my name is tiyana mathew. i am a twelve-year-old future technologist. my goal is to inspire young girls to get into the coding industry.

i got into coding summer of 2020. my sister had seen ads for a workshop called shecodes. i said i was interested, and she signed me up. my family has encouraged me on my coding journey impactfully. especially my sister, hannah yohannan. she has been helping me with my web design skills since i had started coding.

right now, i am in grade six. though i hope to get a degree in computer science in the future and learn some back-end development languages. i am very excited for a bright future ahead.
